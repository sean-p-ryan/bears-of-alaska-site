    ABOUT

    Bears of Alaska are just that: bears from Alaska. They were raised on the rocky banks of the Chukchi Sea by their parents, who were also bears, and upon reaching the age of bear adulthood, decided to assume human form, go to college, and move to New York City.

    Living as humans in separate tiny apartments in Brooklyn, the three former bears (now named Lauren Peters-Collaer, Maria DiFabbio, and Sean Ryan) discovered a love of music, so they decided to put their opposable thumbs and vocal cords to use by learning to play instruments and sing.
